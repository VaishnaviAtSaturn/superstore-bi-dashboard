## Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard
Tool: Power BI | Dataset: 10,000+ sales records (2014–2017)

## Business Questions Answered
- Which region generates highest revenue?
- Which products drive the most profit?
- How do sales trend month-over-month across years?
- Where are sales geographically concentrated?

## Key Insights
- West region leads at $0.73M revenue
- Canon imageCLASS is the highest profit product
- Sales peak in Nov–Dec consistently across all years
- 2017 shows strongest overall growth trajectory

## Tools Used
Python (Pandas) | Power BI | SQL
